# Quickstart Validation Guide: Application Navigation Bar

**Feature**: 005-app-navbar | **Date**: 2026-06-29

## Prerequisites

- Node.js 20+ installed
- Dependencies installed: `npm install` from repo root
- App running locally: `npm run dev` (default port: 3000)

---

## Scenario 1 — Navbar Renders on App Load (FR-001, FR-002, FR-003)

**Goal**: Confirm the navbar is visible immediately on first load.

**Steps**:
1. Open `http://localhost:3000` in a browser.
2. Look at the top of the page.

**Expected outcomes**:
- A horizontal bar is visible at the very top, above the orders list.
- The bar shows "Avify" on the left.
- The bar shows "Comenzi" and "Catalog Produse" links on the right.
- The bar has a white/surface background with a subtle bottom border.

---

## Scenario 2 — Navigation to Orders Page (FR-004, US-1)

**Goal**: Confirm "Comenzi" navigates to the JIRA-style orders list.

**Steps**:
1. Navigate to `http://localhost:3000/catalog`.
2. Click "Comenzi" in the navbar.

**Expected outcomes**:
- The browser URL changes to `http://localhost:3000/`.
- The JIRA-style expandable orders list is displayed.
- No full page reload occurs (client-side navigation).

## Scenario 3 — Navigation to Product Catalog (FR-005, US-1)

## Scenario 4 — Active Link Highlight on Orders Page (FR-006, US-2)

**Goal**: Confirm the correct link is highlighted when on the orders page.

**Steps**:

## Scenario 6 — Navbar Remains Visible on Scroll (FR-007, US-3)

**Goal**: Confirm the navbar sticks to the top when scrolling.

**Steps**:
1. Navigate to `http://localhost:3000/` with several orders present (add orders if needed).
2. Scroll down until the orders list extends past the viewport.

**Expected outcomes**:
- The navbar remains fixed at the top of the viewport throughout the scroll.
- The orders list scrolls underneath the navbar without overlap.
- Both navigation links remain clickable while scrolled down.

---

## Scenario 7 — No Content Obscured by Navbar (SC-005)

**Goal**: Confirm page content starts below the navbar, not underneath it.

**Steps**:
1. Open `http://localhost:3000/`.
2. Observe the top of the page content area.

**Expected outcomes**:
- The first element of the orders list (e.g., the page heading or first order row) is fully visible below the navbar.
- No content is clipped or hidden behind the navbar.

---

## Scenario 8 — Keyboard Navigation (FR-009, SC-004)

**Goal**: Confirm the navbar is fully operable by keyboard.

**Steps**:
1. Open `http://localhost:3000/`.
2. Press `Tab` until focus reaches the navbar links.
3. Press `Tab` again to move between links.
4. Press `Enter` on "Catalog Produse".

**Expected outcomes**:
- Each link receives a visible focus ring when focused.
- Pressing `Enter` navigates to the linked page.
- Focus order follows the visual left-to-right order of links.

---

## Scenario 9 — No Active Highlight on Unknown Route (Edge Case)

**Goal**: Confirm no link is highlighted when the path doesn't match either route.

**Steps**:
1. Navigate to `http://localhost:3000/unknown-page` (will likely show a 404).
2. Observe navbar links.

**Expected outcomes**:
- Neither "Comenzi" nor "Catalog Produse" appears highlighted.
- Both links remain in inactive (muted) style.
